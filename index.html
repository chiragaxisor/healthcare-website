    Cardiology

    Expert heart care delivered by renowned specialists.

    Our Cardiology department is equipped to handle complex heart conditions with advanced diagnostic tools and minimally invasive procedures. We focus on prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of cardiovascular diseases to ensure a healthy heart for every patient.

    Neurology

    Specialized care for the brain, spine, and nervous system.

    Our neurologists provide comprehensive care for patients with neurological disorders such as stroke, epilepsy, and multiple sclerosis. We utilize the latest neuroimaging and therapeutic techniques to optimize patient outcomes.

    Hepatology

    Advanced liver care and treatment.

    We offer specialized services for liver diseases, including hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. Our multidisciplinary team ensures personalized treatment plans including liver transplantation support.

    Pediatrics

    Comprehensive care for infants, children, and adolescents.

    Our pediatric department provides a child-friendly environment for checkups, vaccinations, and treatment of childhood illnesses. We engage parents in the care process to ensure the best health for their little ones.

    Ophthalmologists

    Total eye care solutions for perfect vision.

    From routine eye exams to complex surgeries like cataract and lasik, our ophthalmologists use cutting-edge technology to protect and correct your vision.

    How do I book an appointment?

    You can book an appointment by filling out the form on our website, calling our front desk, or visiting us in person. We also offer online scheduling for registered patients.

    What insurance plans do you accept?

    We accept most major insurance providers. Please contact our billing department or check our accepted insurance list on the "Insurance" page for more details.

    Do you offer emergency services?

    Yes, we have a 24/7 dedicated emergency unit equipped to handle critical medical situations. For immediate life-threatening emergencies, please call 911.

    How can I get my medical records?

    You can request your medical records through our secure patient portal or by visiting the medical records department with a valid ID.

    What specialist services are available?

    We have specialists in Cardiology, Neurology, Pediatrics, Oncology, Dermatology, and more. Please visit our Departments page for a full list.

    Are video consultations available?

    Yes, we offer telehealth and video consultation services for minor ailments and follow-ups. You can select this option while booking your appointment.
